Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 22, 2026
Last Updated: February 22, 2026

Fungometrics ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Fungometrics mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password, date of birth, and profile photo when you register.
  • Player Profile Data: Height, weight, positions played, shirt number, and team affiliation.
  • Performance Data: Batting stats, pitching stats, and session data recorded during use of the App.
  • Contact Information: Mobile phone number, used for team management and SMS notifications.
  • Usage Data: App interactions, session logs, and device information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To display player statistics and performance history
  • To send one-time SMS notifications when a coach adds you to a team roster
  • To manage team rosters and coach–player relationships
  • To improve app performance and user experience
  • To respond to support requests

3. SMS Notifications

When a coach adds a player to a team using the Fungometrics app, the player may receive a one-time SMS notification informing them they have been added. Message frequency is limited to team roster events. Standard message and data rates may apply. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. For help, reply HELP.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Twilio: Our SMS provider, used solely to deliver team notifications to players.
  • Coaches and Team Administrators: Your name, phone number, and performance stats are visible to coaches of teams you belong to.
  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who assist in operating the App under confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your account and data by contacting us.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Children's Privacy

Our App may be used by players under 18 with parental consent. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt out of SMS communications by replying STOP

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
